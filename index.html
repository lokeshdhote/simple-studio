People

& Culture

The studio was founded in 2022 by Guzmán Barquín &
Anita Gutiérrez. Two multidisciplinary designers with 10
years of experience in digital products.

We strongly believe in the power of collaboration and
the idea of growing together by complementing each
other's skills.

We rely on transparency and understand that honest
communication is the way to get things right.

We take pride in knowing that we will always focus on
the well-being and balance of the team, and we truly
enjoy our ability to evolve and adapt along the way.

From Uruguay to Spain

we built Simple Studio® with

a group of people who love what they do.

Simple Studio® Manifesto

We created Simple Studio® believing that there is

always a better way to do things. We are very

passionate about what we do, and we are also the

continuous evolution of the experiences we live.

We value honest design and the processes that drive

it. We trust in the collaboration between people and

how they complement each other.

Above all, we believe in the power of good design.

Simple Studio® Collaborations

We trust in the collaboration between people & companies and
how they complement each other.

Simple Studio® x Zara

We are currently working directly on the design of their
global e-commerce platform, alongside a team
of collaborators dedicated to achieving the best possible user
experience for a platform that has 40 million monthly users.

Simple Studio® x mendesaltaren

We have a very close relationship with the Spanish design
studio mendesaltaren which has allowed us to collaborate
with them on various projects. We also enjoy sharing other
spaces related to professional growth, such as workshops,
training sessions, or talks.

Simple Studio® x Hikko

Hikko, in collaboration with Simple Studio from the very
beginning, is a company dedicated to scaling businesses and
organizations. By synergizing strategic technology with
creative force, they empower ventures to achieve exponential
growth and success.

Simple Studio® x Pento.ai

We helped Pento from the beginning with its positioning,
strategy, and product definition in the Artificial
and Machine Learning industry. Today, the company has
expanded throughout the Americas.

Simple Studio® x ByBeyond

Through a collaboration with ByBeyond, two design studios
with very similar philosophies, processes, and values, we
established an alliance to actively collaborate on new
opportunities.

Simple Studio® x Zenital

One of Uruguay's most promising companies, has a
successful history with 100+ projects in 30 markets.
Leveraging Zendesk, they aim to excel in solving customer
service, experience, and sales challenges with cutting-edge
technology.

Simple Studio® x Ananá

Ananá, a design studio based in Uruguay, has joined forces
with us in a collaborative effort, as both studios share
philosophies, processes, and values, enabling us to explorebr
new opportunities together.

Simple Studio® x DrivenIQ

DrivenIQ came to us with the objective of design
developing both their products and their website. During this
process, we have achieved incredible results, positioning
them successfully in the field of data, intelligence, and large-
scale audiences.
